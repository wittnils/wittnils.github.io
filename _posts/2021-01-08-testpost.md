Let $$K$$ be some field and $$f\in K[X]$$ be separable and $$L/K$$ some splitting field of $$f$$. Then $$f$$ is irreducible if and only if $$\operatorname{Gal}(L/K)$$ acts
transitively on the roots of $$f$$.
